## Description

Your shopping companion has no idea what's coming.

Aggressive store clerks are closing in from every direction — and they won't stop until they've drained every dollar from the budget. Move fast, fight back, and keep your friend alive through three increasingly dangerous stores.

FIGHT BACK WITH RETAIL WEAPONS
Your weapons fire automatically — just keep moving:
• Price Aura — a close-range shock ring that zaps nearby clerks
• Receipts — paper projectiles that lock on to the closest targets
• Barcode Laser — a sweeping beam that carves through the crowd
• Shopping Bag — a burst knockback that clears breathing room

FOUR CLERK TYPES HUNTING YOU DOWN
• Pitcher — relentless pitches, the most common threat
• Closer — slow, patient, devastating budget drain
• Sprinter — dashes in, retreats fast, hard to pin down
• Upseller — amplifies every clerk around it

LEVEL UP AND ESCAPE
Collect XP from defeated clerks, pick upgrades at every level-up, and use coupon lures to distract whole crowds while you slip away.

THREE STORES. ONE ENDLESS MALL.
Beat Electronics Megamart, Fashion Boutique, and Grocery Warehouse to unlock Midnight Mall — the endless survival challenge with no closing time.

COMPETE ON GAME CENTER
Every campaign run tracks your remaining budget. Every endless run tracks how long you lasted. Global leaderboards and achievements for both modes.

MFi and Made for iPhone game controllers supported. Up to 120fps on ProMotion displays.
